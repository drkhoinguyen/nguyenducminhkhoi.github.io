Biography

Khoi Nguyen is a PhD in Computer Science graduating from Oregon State University. His research interests include computer vision and machine learning. His primary research includes 2D/3D understanding, encompassing areas such as detection, segmentation, and reconstruction. In addition, he also explores the realms of Few-shot Learning and Vision-Language Models. Furthermore, Khoi is actively involved in advancing 2D/3D Generation, particularly in Efficient and Trustworthy Generative AI, contributing to push the boundaries of knowledge and innovation.

Interests

  • GenAI including Diffusion Models, Multimodal LLMs
  • Few/Zero-shot/Open-vocabulary Learning and Vision-Language Models
  • 2D and 3D understanding including Detection, Segmentation, and Reconstruction
